About Me

Design. Code. Create.

I'm a graphic designer specialising in branding and digital design, focused on creating clean, purposeful visuals that communicate ideas clearly. My approach is not just about aesthetics — it's about solving problems through design using strong layouts, typography, and visual hierarchy.

In addition to design, I build software products through vibe coding, using AI tools to enhance productivity, explore ideas faster, and develop scalable solutions. I enjoy working at the intersection of design and technology.
